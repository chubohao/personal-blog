METHOD

Logistics Trolley Positioning and Orientation

In the activity area of the logistics trolley, there are a number of 45*45cm large black rubber mats laid out. Each rubber mat is submerged in a RFID tag, while the rubber mat carries magnetic, mat back red arrow points to the North Pole. Logistics trolley has a RFID reader, whenever the logistics trolley passes through the mat word, you can read the mat RFID tags, so you can know where they are located. Logistics trolley can determine the direction of the trolley by judging the magnetic field.

Data Transmission

The logistics system runs on a PC, how do the carts communicate with the system? There is a WiFi module on each hour, and the carts interact with the system through the WiFi module.

Pickup and Unloading

In the activity area of the trolley, you can designate several of them as pickup areas and several others as unloading areas according to your needs (which need to be set through the system software). There is a camera above the pickup area to scan the QR code or barcode of the parcel to determine which unloading port the parcel needs to be sent to.

Route Planning

Route planning is at the heart of the whole system and the core competence of a home company. Therefore, we can't know the specific information. But there are already many excellent path planning algorithms, such as RRT,A*, Elastic band and so on. When dealing with path planning, we first consider collision prevention, and then consider the problem of path shortest.

RESULT

We tested the Libao logistics trolley system in a lab environment, and the results showed that through robotic sorting, each trolley can sort 1,500 packages per day in an 8*6 room, and 10 carts can run on the upper and lower floors.
