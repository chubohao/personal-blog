About Me

Hi, This site is currently in building and is expected to be completed by the end of Oct 2022.

I'm Bohao CHU, graduated from Duisburg-Essen University with a Master's degree in Computer Engineering, supervised by Prof.Dr.Bernd Noche, and graduated from Zhengzhou University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology, supervised by Associate Prof.Dr. Yangjie Cao. Based on my interest and passion for computers, I have worked for a long time as a research assistant in the department of Internet of Things at Zhengzhou University and in the department of Transport Systems and Logistics at Duisburg-Essen University.

Currently, the areas I have explored were mainlyfocused on Interactive Sensing and Applied Machine Learning (Embedded Machine Learning), with a slightinvolvement in PCB, FPGA, and Embedded Operating Systems (Linux, RTOS).
